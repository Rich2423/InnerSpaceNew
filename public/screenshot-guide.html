🛠️ How to Take Screenshots

Method 1: Browser DevTools

  1. Open your app at localhost:3000
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools
  3. Click the device toggle button (📱 icon)
  4. Select device size from dropdown
  5. Navigate to each page and take screenshots
  6. Use Ctrl+Shift+I (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+I (Mac) for full page screenshots

Method 2: Browser Extensions

  • Full Page Screen Capture (Chrome extension)
  • Nimbus Screenshot (Chrome extension)
  • FireShot (Firefox extension)

Method 3: Online Tools

  • BrowserStack - Test on real devices
  • LambdaTest - Cross-browser testing
  • Responsively - Multiple device sizes at once
